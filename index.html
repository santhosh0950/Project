Welcome to Natural Farming

Natural farming promotes chemical-free agriculture, improves soil fertility, protects biodiversity, and produces healthy food naturally.

Natural Farming Practices

Composting

Organic waste is converted into rich compost that improves soil fertility naturally.

Crop Rotation

Growing different crops in sequence prevents soil nutrient loss and controls pests.

Natural Pest Control

Neem and herbal solutions protect crops without harmful chemicals.

Mulching

Covering soil with organic material conserves moisture and reduces weeds.

About Natural Farming

Natural Farming is a sustainable and eco-friendly method of agriculture that avoids the use of chemical fertilizers, pesticides, and synthetic inputs. It focuses on working with nature rather than against it.
