Welcome to Natural Farming

Natural farming promotes sustainable agriculture without chemicals. It improves soil health, protects the environment, and ensures healthy food production.

Natural Farming Practices

Composting

Organic waste is converted into nutrient-rich compost. This improves soil fertility naturally.

Crop Rotation

Different crops are grown in sequence. It prevents soil nutrient depletion and reduces pests.

Natural Pest Control

Neem, herbal sprays and biological methods are used. No harmful chemical pesticides.

Mulching

Soil is covered with straw or leaves. It retains moisture and prevents weeds.

Organic Vegetables

Fresh vegetables grown without chemicals. Healthy for consumers and soil.

Green Farmland

Natural farms maintain biodiversity. They support birds and beneficial insects.

Healthy Crops

Strong crops grown with organic nutrition. Better taste and higher nutrients.

Natural Irrigation

Water is used efficiently and sustainably. Helps conserve natural resources.

About Natural Farming

Natural farming is a chemical-free farming system that focuses on soil health and ecological balance. It reduces cost of cultivation and increases long-term sustainability for farmers.
